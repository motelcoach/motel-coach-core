## The Big Levers: Where We Focus

We don't do everything. We do what moves the needle for a 10-to-40 room independent property.

### 1. The Digital Front Desk (Conversion)
We don't build "brochures." We build high-speed booking engines. If your site doesn't load in under 2 seconds on a patchy regional mobile connection, you are losing money to Booking.com.

### 2. Google Interception & Local SEO
When someone is 10km away and searches "Motel near me," you need to be the #1 choice. We optimize your Google Business Profile to drive phone calls and direct clicks, bypassing the OTA commission.

### 3. The "Fact Layer" for AI & Search
Modern travelers (and AI search engines) want data, not fluff. We replace "beautiful rooms" with "King beds, 50Mbps Wi-Fi, and B-Double parking." High-density info wins the booking.

### 4. Revenue & Rate Integrity
Marketing fails if your pricing is reactive. We help managers implement simple pricing structures that capture peak demand without "discounting" your brand away.
