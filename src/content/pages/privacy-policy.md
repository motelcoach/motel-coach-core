**Privacy Policy**
==================

**Last updated: 24 November 2025**

Motel Coach (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the **Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)** and the **Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)**.  
This policy explains how Motel Coach collects, stores, uses and discloses your personal information, including how we use cookies and tracking technologies for analytics and remarketing.

**1\. Information We Collect**
------------------------------

Motel Coach may collect personal information such as:

*   Name, email, phone number, business or motel details
    
*   Payment and billing information (processed securely by third-party providers)
    
*   Website analytics data (IP address, device type, pages viewed, referral source, behaviour)
    
*   Marketing and advertising data via cookies, pixels, tracking IDs and similar tools
    
*   Course access information, login activity and interactions within our training systems
    
*   Any information voluntarily provided through forms, surveys or support requests
    

**2\. How We Collect Information**
----------------------------------

Motel Coach may collect information through:

*   Forms on our website or LMS
    
*   Purchases and checkout pages
    
*   Email sign-ups and downloadable content
    
*   Cookies and tracking technologies
    
*   Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel and similar tools
    
*   Course enrolment systems and user accounts
    
*   Direct communication with us by email or phone
    

**3\. How We Use Personal Information**
---------------------------------------

Motel Coach uses your personal information to:

*   Provide online training, consultancy, tools and digital downloads
    
*   Deliver course access, account creation and progress tracking
    
*   Process payments securely
    
*   Communicate updates, resources and relevant training information
    
*   Improve our website, sales content and user experience
    
*   Analyse user behaviour and advertising performance
    
*   **Run remarketing and personalised advertising campaigns across Google, Facebook, Instagram and YouTube**
    

**4\. Advertising & Remarketing Compliance (Google, Meta, YouTube, Instagram)**
===============================================================================

Motel Coach uses cookies, pixels, device identifiers and similar tracking technologies to deliver personalised advertising and measure marketing performance.

This includes:

*   **Google Ads Remarketing**
    
*   **Google Analytics Advertising Features**
    
*   **Google Tag Manager**
    
*   **YouTube Remarketing**
    
*   **Meta Pixel (Facebook & Instagram)**
    
*   Additional digital advertising platforms when required
    

These tools help Motel Coach:

*   Build remarketing audiences
    
*   Display personalised ads to previous website visitors
    
*   Measure campaign performance
    
*   Improve relevance of advertising across the internet
    

### **Google Advertising Features**

Motel Coach uses:

*   Google Ads Remarketing
    
*   Google Display Network Impression Reporting
    
*   Google Analytics Demographics & Interests Reporting
    
*   Google Signals (where enabled)
    

Google may use cookies and identifiers to:

*   Show Motel Coach ads based on past interactions
    
*   Measure advertising performance and engagement
    
*   Build anonymised interest segments
    

You may opt out of Google personalised advertising here:  
[**https://www.google.com/settings/ads**](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

### **Facebook & Instagram (Meta) Advertising**

Motel Coach uses the Meta Pixel to:

*   Measure the effectiveness of Facebook/Instagram ads
    
*   Track user actions on our website
    
*   Build Custom Audiences and Lookalike Audiences
    
*   Retarget users with relevant ads
    

Meta’s data policies apply when interacting with these platforms.  
You can adjust your ad preferences here:  
[**https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences**](https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences)

### **YouTube Remarketing**

Motel Coach uses YouTube remarketing tools to:

*   Display personalised ads
    
*   Build custom remarketing lists
    
*   Track behaviour for ad performance
    

Settings can be controlled via your Google account.

### **Cookie & Tracking Control**

Users may manage or disable cookies via their browser.  
Additional opt-out tools:

*   **Network Advertising Initiative:** [https://optout.networkadvertising.org](https://optout.networkadvertising.org)
    
*   **Digital Advertising Alliance:** [https://optout.aboutads.info](https://optout.aboutads.info)
    
*   **Google Ads Opt-Out:** [https://www.google.com/settings/ads](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
    
*   **Meta Ad Preferences:** [https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences](https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences)
    

Disabling cookies may impact the functionality of Motel Coach courses or tools.

**5\. Disclosure of Personal Information**
------------------------------------------

Motel Coach may share information with trusted third-party service providers, including:

*   Payment processors
    
*   LMS and course hosting platforms
    
*   Email marketing providers
    
*   Analytics and advertising platforms
    
*   Cloud hosting and storage providers
    
*   Professional service providers (legal, accounting)
    

Motel Coach does **not** sell personal information.

**6\. Data Security**
---------------------

Motel Coach takes reasonable steps to safeguard personal information through:

*   Secure server environments
    
*   Encryption where appropriate
    
*   Limited access permissions
    
*   Regular monitoring of security practices
    

Some third-party providers may store data overseas.

**7\. Access, Correction & Deletion**
-------------------------------------

You may request:

*   Access to the personal information Motel Coach holds
    
*   Corrections to inaccurate information
    
*   Deletion of your personal data, where legally permitted
    

Contact us at: **contact@motelcoach.com.au**

**8\. Unsubscribe & Marketing Choices**
---------------------------------------

You may unsubscribe from Motel Coach marketing emails at any time via the link in our emails.  
You may also opt out of personalised advertising and cookies as described above.

**9\. Changes to This Policy**
------------------------------

Motel Coach may update this Privacy Policy periodically.  
The most recent version will always be displayed here on our website.
