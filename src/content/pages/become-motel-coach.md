**Become a Motel Coach**
------------------------

Motel Coach creates smart, practical training content for motel and small accommodation operators. We’re looking for experienced professionals to share real-world knowledge across areas like operations, service, marketing, revenue, and maintenance.

### **Why Join?**

*   Reach a global audience of hospitality learners
    
*   Be featured across our website and social media
    
*   Get promotional support for your personal brand
    
*   Contribute at your own pace—remotely or with our team
    

### **What We Look For**

✔ Motel or hospitality experience  
✔ Clear communication  
✔ Friendly, confident presence  
✔ Real insight and willingness to share

### **Ready to Teach?**

If you’re passionate about the industry and want to help others succeed, [apply here](#). We review all submissions and will be in touch if it’s a fit. Know someone great? We’d love a referral too.

Let’s make training better—together.
