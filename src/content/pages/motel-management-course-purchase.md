### Frequently Asked Questions

*   Who is the Course Instructor?
    
    **Benjamin Douglas** is the Lead Instructor at Motel Coach and author of _The Essential Guide to Motel Management_.
    
    His background spans operational and commercial leadership across independent motel and hotel groups, progressing from frontline roles through to executive management overseeing 16+ motels, 10 restaurants, and more than 200 staff.
    
    With experience across regional Australia, Brisbane, and England. Benjamin specialises in profit optimisation, OTA strategy, and practical implementation for independent operators.
    
*   I have no experience, is motel management right for me?
    
    Yes - our training courses are designed to be beginner friendly and to get you ready for day one as a [motel manager](/motel-management).
    
*   I'm an Experienced Manager - what can you offer me?
    
    For our experienced motel managers we focus on optimizing your operation with a combination of operational, marketing and revenue management strategies.
    
*   I'm not good on the computer?
    
    The systems used in motels are very basic. We can get you up to speed in a calm environment using a real motel system before you start your first role.
    
*   How can I contact you?
    
    You can reach us anytime via [contact@motelcoach.com.au](mailto:contact@motelcoach.com.au). We can then schedule a call to discuss your needs.
    
*   Do I get a Certificate?
    
    Yes we provide a certificate of completion to our students when they complete our Foundation and Support Plus courses. Students are required to meet a level of competency, and students struggling will receive additional support.
    
*   What if I don't enjoy the course?
    
    We totally appreciate that not all courses will suit each student. Our courses come with a 10-day money back guarantee.
    
    If you do decide the course is not right for you - we are still here and happy to answer any of your motel related questions and you will still be part of our community.
    
*   Can you help me with my Motel?
    
    Yes, we provide tailored services for motels to increase their performance. Our key focus areas are optimization of existing channels, staffing, marketing and revenue management.
