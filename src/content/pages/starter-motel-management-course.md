Learn Motel Management

#### _No Pressure. Self-Paced. Online Training Course._

### What you don’t need to become a motel manager:

1.  You **don’t need** qualifications or a degree to become a motel manager.
    
2.  You **don’t need** to be a computer power user to become a motel manager.
    
3.  You **don’t need** to be experienced in hotels or motels.
    

### What you do need to become a motel manager:

1.  **A good attitude**, to be friendly and welcoming to everyone that visits your motel. This includes staff. If your staff do not enjoy coming to work, then your job will always be a lot harder.
    
2.  **A worker**, people hiring a motel manager are looking for someone who is willing to work hard, take on responsibilities and tasks when required to ensure the motel runs smoothly. If the housekeepers don’t show up, you jump in and clean rooms, if the maintenance officer doesn’t show up (if you have one), you jump in and fix things within your ability.
    
3.  **A problem solver**, as a motel manager you are in-charge of the operation and smooth running of the business. Most owners will not be on-site, and many will not visit often. Your responsibility is to ensure things work, staff and visitors are safe and the motel makes money. To achieve this you will need to think on your feet.
    
4.  **Dress cleanly and have good hygiene**, motel managers are guest facing, the owner is entrusting you with his reputation and brand. You don’t need to be a model, but every step you can take to be well presented and with a nice smell - will support your career in a motel.
