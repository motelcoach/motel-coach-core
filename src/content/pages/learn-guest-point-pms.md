GuestPoint PMS Course
=====================

### Structured System Training for Independent Motel Operators

GuestPoint is one of the most widely used property management systems across Australian motels. Incorrect setup or inconsistent usage creates operational errors, revenue leakage, and reporting inaccuracies.

This course provides structured, practical GuestPoint training aligned with real motel operations — not generic software tutorials.

[Motel Coach is a recommended consultant and trainer by GuestPoint](https://www.guestpoint.com/resources/relief-managers/).

    Benjamin Douglas is the Lead Instructor at Motel Coach and author of [The Essential Guide to Motel Management.](/motel-management-book)
    
    His background spans operational and commercial leadership across independent motel and hotel groups, progressing from frontline roles through to executive management overseeing 16+ motels, 10 restaurants, and more than 200 staff.
    
    With experience across regional Australia, Brisbane, and London, Benjamin specialises in motel profit optimisation, OTA strategy, and practical implementation for independent operators.

    Yes, the course is self-paced with some instructor led sessions.

    Yes - because we provide a **Certificate of Completion,** we need to ensure all students understand the material before completing the course. We work with students that have not met the required proficiency to ensure all students get through.

*   #### Why This Training Matters
    
    GuestPoint controls:
    
    *   Reservations
        
    *   Room allocation
        
    *   Payments
        
    *   Debtors
        
    *   Housekeeping workflow
        
    *   Daily reconciliation
        
    *   Operational reporting
        
    
    Errors inside the PMS affect revenue, reporting, and guest experience.
    
    This course ensures you operate GuestPoint with confidence and control. It also overlaps and can be completed in conjunction with our motel management course
    
*   #### What You Will Master
    
    System Foundations
    
    Understanding GuestPoint architecture and operational logic.
    
    Reservation Management
    
    Accurate booking entry, modifications, rate control, and operational accuracy.
    
    Group Reservations
    
    Managing allocations and structured group workflows.
    
    Payments & Charges
    
    Processing transactions, handling adjustments, maintaining financial discipline.
    
    Housekeeping Integration
    
    Room status coordination and cleaning workflow control.
    
    Debtors & Accounts
    
    Invoice management and payment tracking.
    
    Reporting & Performance Data
    
    Interpreting system reports to support operational and revenue decisions.
    
    Daily Operational Discipline
    
    End-of-day reconciliation and error prevention.
