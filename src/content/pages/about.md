## From Cleaning Rooms to Managing 16+ Properties

I did not enter hospitality through theory. I built my career from the ground up—cleaning rooms, working reception and handling maintenance tasks inside real motels. I have a practical approach to managing your motel asset that starts with the basics; clean rooms, quality maintenance planning, high quality customer service and effective marketing strategies.

A small motel is a single operating system made up of a 1000 individual levers. If one lever moves incorrectly, performance drops. Motel Coach systematically optimizes your motels performance.

## Real-World Experience
My progression was practical and performance-driven, overseeing more than 200 staff and full operational accountability across:
* **Regional Australia & Brisbane**
* **International Markets (London)**

The scale changes. **The principles do not.** **"A Motel is made up of 1,000 small levers. Profitability isn't lost in one big mistake; it erodes through small misalignments repeated daily."**
