Frequently Asked Questions
==========================

*   ### What is Motel Coach?
    
    Motel Coach is a consulting and training service tailored to independent motels and small hotels. We help owners and managers improve occupancy, streamline operations, boost direct bookings, and increase profitability through practical, no-fluff support.
    
*   ### Who is Motel Coach for?
    
    We work with:
    
    *   Independent motel owners
        
    *   New operators entering the industry
        
    *   Experienced managers looking to refresh operations
        
    *   Small hotel operators managing under 60 rooms  
        Our support is focused on real-world outcomes, not big-brand theory.
        
    
*   ### What services do you offer?
    
    We offer:
    
    *   Online training courses for motel teams
        
    *   Direct booking website development
        
    *   SEO and Google Business Profile optimization
        
    *   OTA performance audits (Booking.com, Expedia, etc.)
        
    *   One-on-one coaching for owners and managers
        
    *   AI Search and digital marketing readiness
        
    *   Reputation and review strategy
        
    
*   ### Do you work with motels outside Australia?
    
    Currently, we focus on Australia and New Zealand markets. However, our methods can be applied globally for similar-size properties.
    
*   ### How do I get started?
    
    You can:
    
    *   Enrol in one of our online courses
        
    *   Book a free consultation
        
    *   Request an OTA or website audit  
        Visit www.motelcoach.com.au to get started.
        
    
*   ### How much does it cost?
    
    Pricing varies by service. We offer:
    
    *   Free downloadable guides and templates
        
    *   One-time project rates (e.g., website, listing audits)
        
    *   Affordable monthly retainers for ongoing support  
        We’re transparent with pricing—no hidden fees or long contract
        
    
*   ### Do I need technical knowledge to use your services?
    
    No. All our materials, tools, and coaching are designed for non-technical motel operators. If you can run a property, you can implement what we teach.
    
*   ### Can you help with direct bookings?
    
    Yes. We specialise in helping motels reduce OTA reliance through:
    
    *   Website design focused on conversion
        
    *   Booking engine integration
        
    *   Rate parity strategies
        
    *   Google visibility and trust signals
        
    
*   ### What’s included in your training courses?
    
    Each course includes:
    
    *   Step-by-step video lessons
        
    *   Downloadable checklists and templates
        
    *   Real-world case studies
        
    *   Action plans you can apply immediately  
        Courses cover key topics like front desk training, marketing, operations, OTA optimization, and digital tools.
        
    
*   ### What makes Motel Coach different from other consultants?
    
    We only work with motels and small hotels—we don’t generalize across the tourism or hospitality sectors. Everything we do is grounded in decades of operational experience, tested in regional and metro markets, and focused on ROI, not theory.
    
*   ### Do you offer custom support or one-on-one coaching?
    
    Yes. We offer personal strategy sessions, training for staff, and tailored marketing or operations consulting. Perfect for properties undergoing change or needing targeted help.
    
*   ### Can you audit my Booking.com or Google listing?
    
    Absolutely. Our audits highlight what's helping or hurting your visibility, ranking, and conversion. You'll get a clear action plan and support implementing changes if needed.
    
*   ### How often do you update your material?
    
    We review all content quarterly to reflect changes in Google, [Booking.com](http://Booking.com), Expedia, and AI search trends. Our goal is to keep you ahead of what’s coming.
    
*   ### Can you help with reviews and reputation management?
    
    Yes. We teach systems for review collection, responses, and using feedback to improve guest experience and boost online scores.
    
*   ### Do you offer services for multi-property operators?
    
    Yes. We support owners and groups with multiple motels/hotels looking to align operations, marketing, and performance across sites.
