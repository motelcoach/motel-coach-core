**Last Edited 23/12/2025.** Most motels don’t have a marketing problem — they have a visibility and conversion problem.

What’s Included in the Free Motel Marketing Audit
-------------------------------------------------

Our **Free Motel Marketing Audit** gives you a clear, practical breakdown of what’s working, what’s holding you back, and where you can make immediate improvements to [drive more bookings](/improve-motel-occupancy).

No sales fluff. No generic advice. Just a straight-talking review based on how guests actually find and book motels today.

### Website Speed & Mobile Performance

Your website is often the final step before a booking. We assess:

*   Mobile load speed and usability
    
*   Core speed issues impacting Google rankings
    
*   Friction points that cause guests to abandon
    

### Direct Booking Optimisation Review

We analyse how effectively your website converts visitors into bookings:

*   Booking engine placement and clarity
    
*   Calls-to-action and rate visibility
    
*   Trust signals, photos, and layout issues
    
*   Missed opportunities to reduce OTA reliance
    

### [Booking.com](/motel-management-blog?category=Booking.com) Listing Review

Booking.com is a primary demand driver for most motels. We review:

*   Listing completeness and content quality
    
*   Photo count, order, and impact
    
*   Room type structure and naming clarity
    
*   Review score trends and visibility risks
    

### Expedia Listing Review

We check how your Expedia presence supports (or weakens) your channel mix:

*   Content alignment with Booking.com
    
*   Rate and availability consistency
    
*   Conversion and presentation issues
    
*   Opportunities to improve exposure without discounting
    

What You’ll Get
---------------

*   A plain-English summary of findings
    
*   Clear priority actions (not a long to-do list)
    
*   Quick wins you can implement immediately
    
*   Strategic recommendations for sustained growth
    

Who This Audit Is For
