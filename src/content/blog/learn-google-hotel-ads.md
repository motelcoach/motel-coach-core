**What Are Google Hotel Ads?**

Google Hotel Ads are a powerful tool that display your motel’s live rates, availability, photos, and a booking link directly on Google Search, Maps, and Google Travel. When someone searches for a motel in your area, they’ll see your property in a price comparison section alongside major online travel agents (OTAs) like Booking.com or Expedia.

**Where Do They Appear?**

*   Google Search results for accommodation-related queries.
    
*   Google Maps, when users look for motels or travel directions.
    
*   Google Travel, Google’s dedicated trip planning platform.
    

Example: A potential guest searches “motels in Mackay” — your motel can appear with price, star rating, and a “Book Now” link under your official name.

**How It Works:**

Google Hotel Ads use a data feed from your Property Management System (PMS) or channel manager to sync your:

*   Rates
    
*   Room availability
    
*   Booking links
    
*   You’ll need to partner with a Google-approved integration partner (like SiteMinder, Cloudbeds, or Little Hotelier) to get started.
    

**Bidding Models (Flexible Payment Options):**

*   Cost-Per-Click (CPC): You pay each time someone clicks your ad.
    
*   Commission Per Stay (CPA): You pay a % only when someone books and stays.
    
*   Pay-Per-Conversion: You pay a % only if someone completes a booking.
    

**Key Benefits for Motel Owners:**

✔ More Direct Bookings – Show your official website with live prices.

✔ Reduced OTA Fees – Compete directly with Booking.com and Expedia.

✔ Increased Visibility – Appear in front of travelers ready to book.

✔ Professional Trust – Your Google listing looks more complete and authoritative.

Is It Worth It for Small Motels?

Yes. Even for small properties, Google Hotel Ads can level the playing field and give you more control over how guests find and book with you. It's especially useful if you have a strong local presence or a loyal customer base.

**Next Steps to Get Started:**

*   Make sure your website supports online bookings.
    
*   Use a booking engine or channel manager that integrates with Google.
    
*   Choose a bidding model that fits your budget.
    
*   Track performance (clicks, bookings, revenue) to optimize your listing.
    

Tip: Keep your Google Business Profile updated with fresh photos, correct amenities, and positive reviews. It boosts your chances of appearing in more searches.
